Mental Health Exercises

  1. Find a comfortable and quiet place to sit or lie down.
  2. Inhale deeply through your nose for a count of 4.
  3. Hold your breath for a count of 4.
  4. Exhale slowly through your mouth for a count of 4.
  5. Repeat this process several times.
  1. Take a moment to reflect on your day.
  2. List three things you are grateful for today.
  3. Consider the positive aspects of your life.
  1. Take a short walk in a quiet environment.
  2. Pay attention to each step as you walk.
  3. Notice the sensations in your feet and legs.
  4. Be aware of the sounds around you.
